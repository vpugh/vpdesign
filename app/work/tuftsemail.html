Tufts University of Dental Medicine Email Series

An email series that was designed and developed to coexist and compliment their existing branding, and be produced quickly and with minimal code changes.

UX/UI, Email Design, Email Development

The Brief

A new partner come on board for partner portals and I was tasked with creating emails that would advertise Tufts University School of Dental Medicine upcoming events. The main task would be to replicate the design and feel of Tufts while making it an easy email to develop in a short time.

Execution

Keeping a united branding I incorporated the already strong branding from the Tufts University School of Dental Medicine website into the emails. I utilized my existing modular email system, this system I developed allowed for customizing visual elements and extending the Tufts brand but maintaining a core email structure. It made replication faster and easier to do on short notice.
